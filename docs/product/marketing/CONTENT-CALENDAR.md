## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### X/Twitter

- **Tone:** Technical but accessible
- **Frequency:** 1-2x daily during launch, 3-5x weekly ongoing
- **Best times:** 9 AM, 12 PM, 6 PM (target audience timezones)
- **Format:** Threads for depth, single tweets for tips
- **Engagement:** Reply to every mention within 2 hours

### LinkedIn

- **Tone:** Professional thought leadership
- **Frequency:** 2-3x weekly
- **Best times:** Tue-Thu, 8-10 AM
- **Format:** Text posts (no links in body), document carousels
- **Engagement:** Comment on industry discussions

### YouTube

- **Tone:** Educational, efficient
- **Frequency:** 2x monthly
- **Format:** Tutorials (5-10 min), demos (2-3 min)
- **SEO:** "markdown editor," "AI writing tool," "Claude Code tutorial"

### Reddit

- **Tone:** Authentic, community-first
- **Subreddits:** r/ClaudeAI, r/Markdown, r/ObsidianMD, r/productivity
- **Rule:** Provide value first, mention product naturally
- **Avoid:** Spammy self-promotion

### Hacker News

- **Tone:** Technical, honest
- **Format:** Show HN (launch), Ask HN (feedback)
- **Rule:** Answer every question thoroughly
- **Timing:** Weekday mornings, avoid weekends
