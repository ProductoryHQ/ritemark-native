# Ritemark Landing Page Copy

**Voice:** Basecamp-style. Direct. Honest. No hype.
**Goal:** 10-100 downloads from people who get it.

---

## The Copy

### Headline

**Write with AI that actually edits your files.**

---

### Subheadline

Stop copying between ChatGPT and your documents. Ritemark has a built-in terminal. Your AI agent edits directly. You review.

---

### The Problem (optional section)

You use AI to write. But the workflow is broken:

1. Write in your editor
2. Copy to ChatGPT
3. Get suggestions
4. Copy back
5. Repeat forever

AI chat can suggest. It can't edit your files.

---

### The Solution

Terminal-based AI agents can. Claude Code. Codex. Gemini CLI. Aider. They read your file, change it, save it.

Ritemark gives you a clean writing app with a terminal built in. One window. Local files. Any agent.

---

### What It Is

- Markdown editor with WYSIWYG
- Built-in terminal (Cmd+`)
- Works offline
- Your files on your machine

---

### What It Isn't

- Not a cloud service
- Not an AI company
- Not trying to replace your AI subscription

You already pay for AI. This is just an editor that works with it.

---

### Get Started

1. Download Ritemark
2. Install any terminal AI agent
3. Open a markdown file
4. Press Cmd+` for terminal
5. Ask your AI to edit

That's it.
