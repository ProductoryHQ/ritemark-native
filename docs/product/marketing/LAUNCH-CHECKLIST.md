## Emergency Playbook

### If: Download Link Broken

1. Fix immediately (highest priority)
2. Update ProductHunt listing
3. Post comment: "Fixed! Sorry for inconvenience"
4. Tweet apology + working link

### If: Critical Bug Reported

1. Acknowledge publicly: "Looking into this"
2. Diagnose (max 2 hours)
3. Ship fix or workaround
4. Update community

### If: Negative Press/Comments

1. Don't be defensive
2. Acknowledge valid criticism
3. Share roadmap for addressing
4. Stay curious: "Can you elaborate?"

### If: Server/Hosting Down

1. Switch to backup hosting
2. Communicate status
3. Post workaround (direct download link)

### If: Competitor Launches Same Day

1. Don't panic
2. Focus on your unique value
3. Avoid direct comparisons
4. Let product speak
