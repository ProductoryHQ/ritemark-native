## LinkedIn

### Post

We just shipped something we are genuinely excited about: voice dictation in Ritemark that runs entirely on your machine.

Here is why this matters:

Most speech-to-text tools send your audio to cloud servers for processing. That means your private notes, journal entries, and drafts pass through someone else's infrastructure.

Ritemark v1.0.3 bundles whisper.cpp - the same technology behind OpenAI's Whisper model - directly into the app. When you click the mic button and start speaking, your words are transcribed locally. No internet connection required. No API calls. No data leaving your machine.

It supports Estonian (our primary market) plus 50+ other languages, with real-time streaming so text appears as you speak.

This is what local-first means to us: powerful features that respect your privacy by default.

Available now for macOS (Apple Silicon): https://ritemark.ee
